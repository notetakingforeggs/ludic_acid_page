About us

Ludic Acid is a collective of makers, movers & mischief‑architects.

We design large‑scale walk‑about puppets and contemporary circus experiences that dissolve the barrier between performer and public. Founded in 2020, we’ve toured festivals across Europe and the UK, enchanting audiences from toddlers to tire‑kickin’ critics.
