About

*Cat Sith* prowls the liminal space between fact and folklore. A towering feline marionette, part circus artist, part mythic creature, she roams festivals and town squares with velvet grace, inviting passers-by into fleeting moments of mischief and wonder. Expect hypnotic aerial silk, unexpected audience interaction and an atmosphere thick with smoke and possibility.

Team

Artistic Director Jamie Smith blends contemporary circus with giant puppet engineering, supported by movement director Shona Keir and visual dramaturg Rowan Grey. Together the collective champions accessible, site–responsive experiences that transform public space into temporary realms of play.
